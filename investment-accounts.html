Traditional IRA & Roth IRA (Tax-advantaged retirement accounts)

  • Fidelity – No account fees, great research & investment options.
  • Vanguard – Best for passive investors; focus on low-cost index funds.
  • Charles Schwab – Strong customer service & ETF selection.
  • E*TRADE – Wide investment options & robust trading tools.
  • Merrill Edge – Good for Bank of America customers with rewards benefits.

401(k), 403(b), 457 (Employer-Sponsored Plans)

✅ Best Plan Providers (Offered through Employers):

  • Fidelity – Most commonly used for employer-sponsored plans.
  • Vanguard – Strong retirement investment options, low-cost funds.
  • TIAA – Common for teachers & non-profit employees (403(b)).
  • Empower (formerly Prudential Retirement) – Popular for government and corporate plans.
  • Charles Schwab – Large employer plans, customizable options.

📌 Note: You can’t open a 401(k) individually; it must be through an employer.

529 Plans (Education Savings Accounts)

✅ Best Platforms (Varies by State Plan):

  • Vanguard (via different state plans) – Low-cost, passive index fund options.
  • Fidelity (State-Sponsored Plans) – Offers 529s for many states.
  • Charles Schwab – Limited availability but solid fund choices.
  • T. Rowe Price – Actively managed fund options.
  • SavingForCollege.com – Good tool to compare state-specific 529 plans.

📌 Note: Most 529 plans are state-sponsored, so the platform depends on which state you choose.

Coverdell ESA (Education Savings Account)

✅ Best Brokers for ESA Accounts:

  • Fidelity – No fees, strong fund selection.
  • TD Ameritrade – Good for actively trading ESA funds.
  • Charles Schwab – Low-cost ETFs & mutual funds for ESAs.
  • Vanguard – Best for passive investing (index funds).

📌 Note: ESAs have fewer provider options than 529 plans.

UGMA/UTMA Custodial Accounts (For minors, converts at adulthood)

✅ Best Brokers for Custodial Accounts:

  • Fidelity Youth Account – Best overall, no fees, fractional shares.
  • Charles Schwab – Custodial accounts with strong investment choices.
  • TD Ameritrade – Good for more active investors.
  • E*TRADE – Low-cost ETFs & stocks for kids' investing.
  • Greenlight + Invest – Great for beginners (kid-friendly interface).
